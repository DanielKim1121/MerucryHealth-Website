How it Works

Our team easily installs the Mercury device in a desired room

The Mercury device then begins to capture video of the room

It sends the video to our servers which anonymously analyzes the data to look for patterns in person's movement

If we recognize a certain movement, such as a person falling, we send a notification to a predefined emergency contact or emergency services

Our Device

Non-Wearable

Unlike current technologies which use wearable technology (like the Apple Watch™ and Life Alert) to analyze person actions, our device does it entirely from a distance.

Position Recognition

With a small, unintrusive camera our device can detect elderly falls and other human actions in real time.

Emergency Alert

Have peace of mind while your loved one lives safely at home with the independence they desire. Receive a message instantly if anything is wrong.
